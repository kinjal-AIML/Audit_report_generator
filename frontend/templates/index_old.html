Detection Patterns
1
Sudden Balance Spike
15 pts
Fields Used (L2,L3)
L2: account_balance, L3: avg_txn_last_30days
Example: Avg balance ₹20,000 → suddenly ₹5,00,000
2
Rapid Pass-Through
30 pts
Fields Used (L1)
L1: txn_timestamp, amount, transaction_type
Example: >=3 outgoing txns within 5 minutes after large credit
3
Fan-Out Distribution
20 pts
Fields Used (L1)
account_id, counterparty_account
Example: One account sends money to ACC101, ACC102, ACC103, ACC104, ACC105 (≥5 unique receivers)
4
New Account High Volume
20 pts
Fields Used (L1,L2)
L1: amount, L2: account_open_date
Example: Account opened 3 days ago, total turnover ₹8,00,000
5
Structured Transfers
15 pts
Fields Used (L1)
L1: amount, L1: txn_timestamp
Example: 10 transactions of ₹49,000 within 1 hour
6
Incomplete KYC
25 pts
Fields Used (L2)
kyc_status
Example: KYC = "pending" but account does high-value transfers (₹5L+)
7
Multi-Country Activity
20 pts
Fields Used (L2)
country
Example: Transactions from 5+ different countries in 30 days
8
Shared Device Usage
30 pts
Fields Used (L2)
device_id
Example: Same phone used by 5 accounts
9
Shared IP Address
20 pts
Fields Used (L2)
ip_address
Example: 10 accounts using same IP
10
Failed Login Spike
10 pts
Fields Used (L3)
failed_login_attempts
Example: 7 failed attempts → then login → transfer
